Introduction

The Continuous Job Network (CJN) is an innovative initiative aimed at bridging the gap between job seekers, educational institutions, and employers. With a focus on real-time job opportunities, skill enhancement, and audience engagement, CJN offers a comprehensive platform for students, professionals, and recruiters. Powered by Smart Cookie Rewards and in collaboration with AICTE, CJN is redefining the way career services are delivered.

Why CJN?

  • Real-Time Job Access: Seamlessly connects job seekers with live openings and interviews.
  • Skill Development: Offers career advice and guidance for employability enhancement.
  • Corporate Branding: Provides a platform for companies to showcase culture and values.
  • Holistic Approach: Integrates user feedback, gamified rewards, and interactive tools to foster engagement.

How Does CJN Look?

  • TV Screen Dashboard: Features stats, audience interaction tools, and an advertisement bar for promotions.
  • Mobile and Tablet Modes: The CJN Companion App provides two user-friendly modes for easy accessibility.
  • Login Entities: Viewer, Candidate/Student, and Company roles ensure streamlined interaction.

Key Features and Delivery Methods

  • Jobs and Interviews: Real-time presentations and interviews.
  • Resume Management: Simplified uploading and updating of resumes.
  • Dream Job Insights: Expert advice for job seekers and recruiters.
  • Broadcast Channels: Utilizes traditional TV, campus networks, AICTE, and UGC channels.
  • Digital Platforms: Leverages Internet TV and Swayam Prabha for broad access.

Interactive Engagement Strategies

  • AI for Real-Time Feedback: Enhances user experience by adapting to preferences and feedback.
  • Protsahan Mudra Rewards: Incentivizes user participation with gamified rewards.
  • Companion App: Facilitates seamless interaction and real-time feedback.

Educational Integration and NEP 2020

  • On-the-Job Training (OJT): Enables students to gain practical experience.
  • Hobby Nurturing: Encourages students to explore and develop their interests.
  • College Clubs: Promotes content creation and leadership development within institutions.

Process Flow and Vision

  • Job Descriptions: Curated and published with summaries.
  • Video Content: Professionally recorded, edited, and quality-checked.
  • Broadcast and Promotion: Shared on campus TV, social media, and YouTube.
  • Feedback Loop: Continuously improved through user input and analytics.

Workforce Details

  • Technology Experts: Manage platforms, streaming, and AI integration.
  • Content Creators: Develop engaging video and textual materials.
  • Marketing and Outreach: Drive engagement through strategic promotions.
  • Production Team: Includes camera operators, editors, and directors for high-quality outputs.

AICTE Collaboration

CJN leverages its Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with AICTE to implement initiatives across educational institutions. Over 2,000 AICTE-affiliated colleges have adopted the Smart Cookie Rewards platform for 360-degree feedback and career services.

Sample Show Ideas

  • Career Advice: Expert guidance for job seekers.
  • Industry-Specific Careers: Insights into niche fields.
  • Personal Branding: Skill development for standing out in the job market.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: Addressing workplace equity and belonging.

Future Scope

  • Creating dedicated CJN clubs in colleges.
  • Integrating advanced AI tools for interaction.
  • Hosting live webinars and events for skill-building.
