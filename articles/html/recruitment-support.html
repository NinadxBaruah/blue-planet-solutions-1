Recruitment Process Support Services

Introduction

Blue Planet Solutions Inc. (BPSI) provides a scalable Recruitment Process Support (RPS) model that facilitates the challenging process of identifying and selecting the best available talent. With BPSI’s process working behind the scenes, your team can focus on sales and client support, while we handle the rest.

Corporate Presence

  • Global Operations: Offices in the USA (NY) and India (New Delhi, Pune, Nagpur, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai).
  • Expert Workforce: Dedicated teams to provide quality recruitment services.

Core Recruitment Support Services

Daily Support Mode

Searching and screening resumes on leading job boards such as Dice, Net-temps, LinkedIn, Monster, and more. We ensure potential candidates are shortlisted and communicate with them via various channels.

Over-Night Resume Express®

Provide candidates overnight through our specialized express services for time-sensitive recruitment needs.

Week-End Resume Express®

Get resumes over the weekend, ensuring your recruitment needs are always met, even during off-hours.

Resume Database Refresh

Regularly refreshing the resume database by calling candidates and ensuring the availability of up-to-date profiles.

Passive Candidate Searches

Searching for passive candidates through LinkedIn, Meetup, GitHub, and more to ensure the best talent pool.

H1 Process Management

Managing the H1 visa process efficiently, ensuring smooth candidate placement and compliance.

VMS Support Services

Providing Vendor Management System (VMS) support to streamline candidate submissions and job order management.
